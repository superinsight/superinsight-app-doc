# User Profile

This section explains how to view and manage your user profile settings in Superinsight.

## Accessing Your Profile

To access your user profile:

1. Click on your **profile icon** in the top right corner of the application.
2. Select **Profile** from the dropdown menu.

## Profile Settings

Your user profile contains the following information:

### Personal Information

- **Name**: Your display name in the application
- **Email**: Your registered email address

### Account Settings

- **Password**: Change your account password
- **Notification Preferences**: Manage email and in-app notifications

## Updating Your Profile

To update your profile information:

1. Navigate to your profile settings.
2. Click **Edit** next to the section you want to update.
3. Make your changes.
4. Click **Save** to confirm your updates.

If you need further assistance with your profile settings, please contact our **Live Help** support.

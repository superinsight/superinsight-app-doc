# Manage Setting

This section explains how to manage your settings in Superinsight.

## Accessing Settings

To access your settings:

1. Click **Settings** in the sidebar
2. You will see two tabs: **User Settings** and **Legal & Compliance**

## User Settings

Manage your personal account settings and preferences.

## Legal & Compliance

View and download legal documents. If you need to download the Business Associate Agreement (BAA) or view other legal documents, you can find them in the **Legal & Compliance** tab.

### Legal Documents

#### Terms of Use

Terms and conditions for using our platform.

[View Terms of Use](terms-of-use.md)

#### Privacy Policy

How we collect, use, and protect your data.

[View Privacy Policy](privacy-policy.md)

#### HIPAA Notice of Privacy Practices

Notice regarding health information privacy.

This notice describes how medical information about you may be used and disclosed and how you can get access to this information.

### HIPAA Compliance

#### Business Associate Agreement (BAA)

Required document establishing permitted uses and disclosures of PHI.

A Business Associate Agreement (BAA) is a legally binding document required under HIPAA that establishes the permitted uses and disclosures of Protected Health Information (PHI) between a covered entity and a business associate.

- **Data Protection** - Security measures for handling PHI

- **Privacy Safeguards** - Procedures to protect patient privacy

- **Breach Notification** - Protocols for reporting security incidents

For any questions regarding our HIPAA practices or to exercise your privacy rights, please contact us at [help@superinsight.ai](mailto:help@superinsight.ai).
